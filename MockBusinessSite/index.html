Our Promises:

High-Quality Pick-Me-Ups, Every Time

Our roasters and baristas strive to create the best drinks possible, every step of the way. From inspecting our green beans, to roasting them to the perfection, to grinding them fresh for every shot of espresso. Our staff have all been specially-trained in the art of coffee mixology and are all prepared to deliver whatever your heart desires (within reason, of course)!

Intuitive, Helpful Staff

Every barista and staff member understands our menu inside and out, and they're all here to help you navigate to your new favorite beverage. Whether it be something green (rose-infused matcha with coconut milk, on ice?) or a classic, deep-brown cuppa joe (you can't go wrong with a classic). If you're ever unsure, we love playing 20-Questions to tune in and help you to leave happy!

A Family-Friendly Environment

We understand that many of our customers are juggling youngsters and teens, alike. All are welcome at Cafe Promise, and we're happy to accomodate kiddos of all ages with pleasantries like board games, playing cards, and less-mess arts-and-crafts, so bring the whole family and relax, knowing there's something for everyone!
