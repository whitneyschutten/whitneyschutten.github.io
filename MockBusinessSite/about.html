About Cafe Promise

How it all Started

Have you ever been out and about, wandered by a cafe and thought to yourself, "I could use a drink, why not?" Then, to your dismay, what you received was not up to par with your standards? Or the customer service ruined the entire experience for you? I'd like to think we've all been there... and most of us, here at Cafe Promise have certainly been there more than we'd like to admit!

Well, we started a mission to prevent that from happening (at least on our block!), and one small step at a time, Cafe Promise was born! It started with a small group of baristas who wanted to offer something better than what was available, in any way we could. This meant starting small (think farmer's markets), but our popularity quickly grew!

Over time, we built up the network and resources to lease our very own space, then, through lots of love and elbow-grease, beautified it and prepared it for the masses! Years have passed and the love in our little business has only grown! We're passionate about the drink-business, and we're passionate about maintaining a safe, happy, and tolerant space!

We firmly believe that it's this set of values that sets us apart from the big coorporate shops. We focus on the small and local, on the people, rather than just the numbers. We hope you feel the difference, too, and return time and time again to take a break from the outside world with us. We promise to keep our values the same as the days go on. Welcome, friends!

Meet Our Staff

We understand that coffee can get personal, and your relationship with your barista can be a big part of that! Get to know these wonderful individuals and feel the familiarity the moment you reach the counter!

Whitney

"The Impulsive One" -owner & operator

She worked for nearly a decade with multiple coffee shops in her town, alongside waiting tables. After hitting the ceiling at various locations, she needed a change. If she wanted a boss who engaged with her employees and the business, a dynamic that was healthy for everyone on board, she would have to BE that boss (boss is a 4-letter word, let's say "team leader").

Dennis

"The Practical One" -owner & operator

Born and raised in The Netherlands, Dennis hopped over the pond (The Atlantic, actually) to be with the love of his life. Little did he know, he would be knee-deep in moments of "helping out" at his wife's new business! While they promised to never work in the same place at the same time, this proved to be a happy change-in-plans. With his customer-service expertise and love for carpentry, Dennis found a happy place with us (thank goodness!).

Elena

"The Fun One" -Head Barista

This ray of sunshine is always a joy to interact with! Elena has many years of customer-service behind her, and is incredibly intuitive when it comes to playing 20-questions. Not sure what you would like? Ask our head-barista for some guidance, and she'll lead the way. Feeling shy? Don't worry, she's a pro at making anyone and everyone feel welcome and at home. Really, we love her.
